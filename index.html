Revolutionizing Micro Wind Generation for Electric Cars

Hydragon Energy LLC is a small business dedicated to developing and commercializing micro wind generation systems for electric cars. Our technology is designed to be compact, efficient, and cost-effective, providing a sustainable and renewable source of energy for the growing electric vehicle market.

Our Solutions

Compact and Efficient Micro Wind Generation Systems

Our micro wind generation systems are designed to be installed on the roof of electric cars, providing a clean and renewable source of energy to supplement the car's battery. Our technology is compact, lightweight, and produces minimal drag, making it an ideal solution for electric cars of all shapes and sizes.

Research and Development Funding

Hydragon Energy LLC has received funding from the National Science Foundation to support research and development of our micro wind generation systems. We are committed to leveraging this funding to create innovative and impactful solutions for the electric vehicle market.

About Us

Hydragon Energy LLC is a startup company focused on developing micro wind generation systems for fleet vehicles and electric vehicle manufacturers. Our technology has the potential to revolutionize the clean energy industry by providing a renewable source of energy for electric vehicles, reducing their reliance on traditional power sources and minimizing their carbon footprint. We have already built a working prototype and are seeking seed funding through the NSF Seed Fund Grants program to bring our product to market.
